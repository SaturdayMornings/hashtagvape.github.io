Welcome!

What is the difference between people who talk about cigarettes, vapes, and Ecigs on Twitter? We've analyzed about 75,000 tweets from users who use the hashtags #Ecigs, #cigarettes, and #vape to find out. The journey began when we:

  1. Crawled twitter for Tweets containing the termss #Ecigs, #cigarettes, and #vape
  2. Took every user handle that was present in the collected tweets, and collected each and every single one of their posts to understand their habits and popularity.
  3. Analyzed these users and copared them on the following attributes:
    • Number of followers
    • Statuses count
    • Retweet count
What did we approach this experiment expecting to find?
